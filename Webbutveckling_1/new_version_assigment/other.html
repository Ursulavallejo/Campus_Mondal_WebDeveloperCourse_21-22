Bomba Estéreo:

AMANECER

Inspelat mellan LA och Bogotá är Amanecer första gången electro-cumbia-tungviktare Bomba Estéreo har tagit in externa producenter: främst Ricky Reed, som producerade Jason Derulos ”Talk Dirty” och arbetade med Pitbulls globalisering. Albumet innehåller vallmo i ansiktet och ögonblick som med rätta kan märkas EDM.

"Amanecer is an album by Bomba Estéreo. It earned Bomba Estéreo a Latin Grammy Award nomination for Best Alternative Music Album and a Grammy Award nomination for Best Latin Rock, Urban or Alternative Album. It peaked at number 17 on the Billboard Top Latin Albums chart. It was also considered the best Latin album of 2015 by Rolling Stone.

It includes the viral hit "Soy Yo", which features Costa Rican child star Sarai Gonzalez, and received over 109 million views on YouTube.[4] The song and video were called a "celebration of self and a symbol of proud Latinidad" and "an ode to brown girls"" WIKIPEDIA
