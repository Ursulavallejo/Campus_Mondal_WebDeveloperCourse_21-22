Foster home

Fostering a dog is an incredibly rewarding experience that really makes you grow as a person. The difference you can make through fostering is immeasurable.Here are some things you should know before taking in a foster:

  • You need to ensure that you have the time, energy, and money to look after a dog until they find their forever home. Your lifestyle may have to change.
  • You might be a dog’s first ever home and each dog will have different training needs, like how to walk on a leash or be alone without getting anxious.
  • Make sure your home is suitable and safe for a dog, and please check with your landlord first.

The thought of having a new bestie is super exciting, but make sure you consider everything thoroughly. Ask the shelter for information about the dog’s personality/needs, so that you and the dog can have the best possible experience. Communication is key and you need to make sure it’s right for you too.
